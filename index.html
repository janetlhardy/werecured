The Story Behind the Art

Photo

I've been making things for as long as I can remember. Over the years my hands have been busy with painting, ceramics, polymer clay, wire jewelry, mosaics in every medium imaginable — tesserae, mirror, eggshells — and I'm also a master knitter. Resin found me when I was looking for my next obsession, and let's just say I'm cured. I live in beautiful Victoria, BC with my husband and a very important dog.
